abstract: |
  This HAICON 2026 poster presents self-supervised deep learning methods for denoising raw tomography detector data from ultrafast electron beam X-ray computed tomography. The work compares deep-learning-based denoising against a conventional non-learning baseline and evaluates image quality at both the sinogram and reconstruction levels using PSNR, SSIM, CTF, and MTF. The results show improved signal-to-noise ratios and clearer reconstructed images, supporting self-supervised denoising as a practical route for AI-assisted scientific imaging workflows.
