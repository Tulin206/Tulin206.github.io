## S³ School 2026 Recap

I attended the S³ School 2026 training on sustainable scientific software and later gave a recap presentation to share the key practices with colleagues.

<!--more-->

The presentation focused on sustainable scientific software engineering for research environments, with practical guidance on reproducible workflows, Python project structure and packaging, Git/GitHub collaboration, Pixi environments, testing, CI/CD, profiling and optimization, containerization, software quality, security-aware development, and archival publication using Zenodo and Software Heritage.

This activity reflects both my training in sustainable scientific software and my ability to communicate research software practices clearly to technical audiences.
