abstract: |
  This talk introduces the HPC Gateway initiative — a Helmholtz-funded effort bridging academic HPC research and industrial innovation across nine research centers. It covers two key topics: AI-based denoising of ultrafast X-ray CT sinogram data from ROFEX using deep learning, demonstrating improved reconstruction quality over traditional approaches; and CFD-based process optimization with automated workflows and recommender systems for industrial multiphase flows. The presentation shows how HPC, AI, and domain expertise can accelerate digitalization and strengthen industry–research collaboration.
